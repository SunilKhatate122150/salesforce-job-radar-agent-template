Speaking Drills — Vocal Authority
Exercises to improve pace, clarity, and the "Senior Developer" tone of voice.
Drill 01
The Power Pause
Before answering ANY technical question, take a full 2-second pause. Look at the interviewer, breathe, then start.

Why? It signals that you are thinking, not just reciting. It makes your first sentence sound significantly more authoritative.
Drill 02
70% Speed Execution
Pick a complex topic (e.g., Order of Execution). Explain it out loud at exactly 70% of your normal speaking speed.

Why? Nervousness makes you speak fast. Fast speaking makes you trip over words and sound junior. Slowing down to 70% makes every word clear and sounds like deep expertise.
Drill 03
End Sentence Snap
Practice ending your answers without trailing off (avoiding "...so yeah," or "...basically that's it.").

Target Phrase: "...That is the core principle I follow in all my Apex development." or "...Does that cover what you were looking for, or should I go deeper into the code?"
Daily Speaking Routine (15 mins)
  • 0-5 min: Read one Salesforce blog post ALOUD with 100% pronunciation focus.
  • 5-10 min: Record your self-intro. Listen to it. Identify ONE filler word (um, basically, like).
  • 10-15 min: Redo the intro without that specific filler word.
