Vocabulary & Phrases — Senior Authority
Upgrade your technical language from "functional" to "architectural."
Senior Sentence Starters
  • "The way I approach architecture for this is..."
  • "In a high-volume production environment, what I've found is..."
  • "There is a significant tradeoff to consider here..."
  • "From a maintainability standpoint, I prefer..."
  • "The primary bottleneck we encountered was..."
Technical Power Words
  • Bulkification: Instead of "processing many records."
  • Decoupling: Instead of "separating things."
  • Asynchronous: Instead of "running in the background."
  • Scalability: Instead of "handling more data later."
  • Governor Limits: Instead of "Salesforce restrictions."
Domain Specific (Mortgage)
  • Loan Origination System (LOS): The core platform.
  • TRID/Compliance: The regulatory framework.
  • Third-party Integration: DocuSign, Experian, etc.
  • Data Integrity: Ensuring mortgage data is accurate.
