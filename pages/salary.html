Salary & Negotiation — Senior Strategy
How to handle the money conversation with confidence and secure your market value.
The "Current Salary" Trap
The Strategy
In India, HR often asks for your current CTC and "fixed vs variable" breakdown early.

The Script: "I'm focusing on the value I bring to this specific role and the market rate for Senior Developers with my specific domain expertise. Based on my research for a role of this complexity in Pune, I'm looking at a range of [Your Range]. Does that align with your budget for this position?"
Handling Low-Ball Offers
  • "I'm very excited about the team and the project. However, the current offer is a bit below what I'm seeing in the market for my 4 years of specialized mortgage experience. Is there any flexibility in the base component?"
  • "If we can get the fixed component to [Target], I'd be ready to sign immediately. I'm confident my ability to hit the ground running on your integrations will provide immediate ROI."
Market Context (Pune 2026)
For 4-5 years experience with LWC, Integration, and Domain expertise:
  • Strong: 18 - 24 LPA
  • Exceptional (Product/US MNC): 25 - 32 LPA
  • Notice Period: 90 days is standard, but having a "buy-out" option or being in your last 30 days significantly increases your leverage.
