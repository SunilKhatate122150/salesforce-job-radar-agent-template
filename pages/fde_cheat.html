Instruction hierarchy: Agent vs Topic vs Action InstructionsCONCEPT▼
  • Agent Instructions (System Prompt) — Global persona + guardrails. Every conversation. "You are a helpful mortgage assistant. Never provide legal advice."
  • Topic Instructions — Specific to one domain. "Always verify identity before sharing loan details."
  • Action Instructions — Tell Atlas HOW/WHEN to invoke an action. "Call getLoanBalance when customer asks about balance or payoff."
  • Priority: Agent → Topic → Action. Lower-level wins within scope but can't override agent-level guardrails
Analogy

"Think of it like: Agent = company policy, Topic = department rules, Action = specific procedure."
